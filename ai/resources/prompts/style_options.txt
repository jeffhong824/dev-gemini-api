# Available Style Options for Image Generation

## Predefined Styles
- photorealistic: High-quality realistic photography style
- artistic: Creative artistic interpretation
- cartoon: Cartoon/animation style
- anime: Japanese anime/manga style
- oil_painting: Traditional oil painting style
- watercolor: Watercolor painting style
- sketch: Pencil/charcoal sketch style
- digital_art: Digital art/illustration style
